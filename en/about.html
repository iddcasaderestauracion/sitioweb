Our mission and vision

Mission

To be a spiritual home where every person finds restoration, purpose, and a faith family to walk alongside them with God.

Vision

To be a house of restoration that impacts our community and beyond, forming disciples who transform their homes, workplaces, and cities.

What we believe

We believe in a gospel that transforms lives: in God's grace, the restoring power of His Spirit, and in the church as a family that walks together.
